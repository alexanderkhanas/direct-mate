Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

DirectMate ("we", "our", "us") operates an AI-powered sales assistant that helps fashion and beauty stores manage customer conversations on Instagram. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Instagram Messages: We receive and process messages sent to connected Instagram business accounts to provide automated responses.
  • Account Information: Instagram account IDs, page tokens, and business profile information necessary to operate the service.
  • Product Data: Product catalog and inventory data synced from connected e-commerce platforms (Shopify, OpenCart).
  • Usage Data: Conversation logs, response metrics, and service performance data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To receive and respond to Instagram Direct Messages on behalf of connected business accounts.
  • To provide product recommendations and availability information to customers.
  • To manage reservations and order workflows.
  • To improve our AI response quality and service reliability.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share information only as necessary:

  • With Meta/Instagram APIs to send and receive messages.
  • With AI providers (OpenAI) to generate responses — messages are sent without personally identifiable information where possible.
  • With connected e-commerce platforms to sync product and order data.

4. Data Retention

Conversation data is retained for the duration of the business relationship. Businesses may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption of access tokens (AES-256-GCM), secure HTTPS connections, and access controls to protect stored data.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Businesses using DirectMate can manage their data through the admin panel.

7. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us at privacy@directmate.app. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@directmate.app.
